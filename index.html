About Us

Jeffrey L. Schild is the owner and president of Schild Electric, Inc. Born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, he graduated from Bucknell University in 1992 with a BS in electrical engineering. Jeff has been working in the construction and contracting world his whole career (25+ years). He has been a project manager for various companies (Johnson Controls and Gordon Contractors to name a few) installing control systems, electrical, mechanical (piping and sheet metal), lighting, roofing, waterproofing, fire and security systems. His jobs have taken him across the United States (PA, OH, NC, VA, MD, ND, TX, CO, FL, NM, WY and Washington DC). Projects involved large industrial sites, commercial buildings and residential homes. Before starting Schild Electric, Jeff was working as a senior electrical engineer for an engineering firm in the gas and oil industry. For the past couple of years, Jeff has been working with general contractors and foundation repair contractors in the DFW Metroplex, bringing his years of experience to solve electrical problems and install electrical solutions on their projects.
